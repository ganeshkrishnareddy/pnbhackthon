Why QuantumGuard?

Quantum computers will soon break modern encryption (Shor Algorithm). "Store Now, Decrypt Later" attacks are already happening. Here is how we protect you.

What We Do

QuantumGuard is a state-of-the-art vulnerability scanner designed specifically for the impending quantum threat. We perform non-intrusive, deep-packet analysis of your public-facing APIs, VPNs, and domains. By assessing your TLS handshakes, certificate authorities, and key-exchange mechanisms, we map your entire cryptographic landscape without a single hour of downtime.

We don't just find vulnerabilities; we predict them with AI, establish CBOMs (Cryptographic Bill of Materials), and provide you with actionable steps to migrate to quantum-resistant algorithms (like Kyber and Dilithium) seamlessly.

The Threat Landscape

  • 🔴 Harvesting Attacks: Hackers intercept encrypted traffic today to decrypt it tomorrow.
  • 🔴 RSA/ECC Depreciation: Standard PKI infrastructure will become completely obsolete.
  • 🔴 Compliance: Regulatory bodies (NIST) are mandating PQC migrations now.
