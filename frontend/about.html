Our Mission

With the rapid advancement in quantum computing, traditional encryption methods like RSA and ECC are on the verge of becoming obsolete. Our mission is to democratize post-quantum cryptography (PQC) readiness. We provide organizations of all sizes with enterprise-grade tools to assess their cryptographic posture, generate accurate CBOMs, and transition to quantum-safe algorithms before "Harvest Now, Decrypt Later" attacks compromise their most sensitive data.

Privacy Policy

At QuantumGuard, we take your data privacy seriously. As a security-first organization, we process scan requests momentarily in memory. We do not permanently store sensitive infrastructure metadata or PII without explicit consent.

Any CBOM data generated is restricted entirely to the authorized user's session and encrypted in transit. We comply with major data regulations.
